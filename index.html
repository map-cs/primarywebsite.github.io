Mission Aligned Programs for Computer Science (MAP-CS)

MAP-CS is an NSF-supported project (Award #2517967) focused on understanding and documenting how computer science programs can be intentionally aligned with the missions of liberal arts institutions.

This site shares project goals, tools, and findings for faculty and institutions interested in mission-aligned CS education.
